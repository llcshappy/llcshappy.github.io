Biography

I am an algorithm expert at Alpsentek, working on algorithms and applications built around EVS (event-based vision) for ultra-low-power sensing. Previously I was perception PL for the Xiaoyao line at Narwal (semantic segmentation, stereo depth, and end-to-end upstream/downstream integration). At SenseTime I focused on Remosaic and broader image-quality (IQ) algorithms for mobile ISP pipelines. My background is in automation from Tsinghua (M.Eng.) and HUST (B.Eng.).
